# SocietySync User Guide

Welcome to SocietySync! This guide will help you navigate the mobile app and make the most out of its features.

---

## 1. Installation & Login

1. **Download the App:** Install SocietySync on your Android or iOS device.
2. **Open the App:** You will be greeted by the Splash Screen followed by the Invite Code screen.
3. **Invite Code:** Enter the Invite Code provided by your society admin.
4. **Phone Number & OTP:** Enter your registered mobile number. You will receive an SMS with a 6-digit OTP. Enter this OTP to complete the login process.

---

## 2. Navigating the Home Screen

Once logged in, you will see the Resident Home Screen. Key features include:
- **Welcome Banner:** Displays your name and Flat number.
- **New Complaint Button:** Quick access to lodge a personal flat complaint.
- **Report Society Issue Button:** Quick access to report problems in common areas (parks, hallways, etc.).
- **My Ironing Bills Button:** View your outstanding and past ironing requests and dues.
- **Active Complaints:** A list of your ongoing complaints and their real-time status.

---

## 3. Creating a Complaint

To request a repair or service for your flat:
1. Tap **New Complaint**.
2. **Select Category:** Choose the relevant category (Electrical, Plumbing, Housekeeping, etc.).
3. **Describe the Issue:** Provide clear details about the problem.
4. **Preferred Visit Time:** Select when you want the worker to visit (e.g., Morning, Evening, Any Time, or specify a Custom Time).
5. **Attach Image (Optional):** Tap the Camera or Gallery button to upload a photo of the issue. This helps the worker understand the problem before arriving.
6. **Submit:** Tap Submit. You can track its status on the home screen.

---

## 4. Reporting a Society Issue

If you spot a problem in a shared area (e.g., a broken gym machine or a flickering streetlight):
1. Tap **Report Society Issue**.
2. Enter a **Title** and a detailed **Description**.
3. **Attach Image (Optional):** Snap a quick picture of the issue.
4. **Submit:** The issue is directly routed to the Admin Dashboard for society-wide resolution.

---

## 5. Tracking and History

- **Active Complaints:** Tap on any active complaint on your home screen to view its timeline, assigned worker details, and any notes left by the worker (e.g., "Need to buy spare parts").
- **View History:** Tap the "View History" button on the home screen to see a log of all your past, resolved, or closed complaints.

---

## 6. Notifications

- Tap the bell icon in the top right corner of the home screen to access your **Notifications**.
- Here you will receive updates when a worker's status changes, when a complaint is resolved, or when the Admin sends a society-wide broadcast.
