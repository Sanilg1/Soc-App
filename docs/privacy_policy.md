# Privacy Policy for SocietySync

**Last Updated:** June 2, 2026

Welcome to SocietySync. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and admin dashboard (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
- **Contact Information:** Phone numbers, flat/apartment numbers, and names provided during registration or by the society administrator.
- **Role Information:** Your assigned role in the society (Resident, Worker, Admin, Guard).
- **Authentication Data:** OTP codes and session data for authentication.

### B. Usage Data and Content
- **Complaints & Issues:** Descriptions, categories, and photos you upload when reporting a complaint or society issue.
- **Service Requests:** Data related to worker visits, ironing requests, and billing records.
- **Images:** Photos you voluntarily upload from your camera or gallery to attach to issues. We only access images you explicitly select.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **Providing the Service:** To route your complaints to the correct workers and administrators.
- **Communication:** To send you notifications regarding the status of your complaints or society-wide announcements.
- **Security:** To authenticate users and ensure only verified residents and workers have access to the society's platform.
- **Improvement:** To monitor service Level Agreements (SLAs) and improve society management operations.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information in the following circumstances:
- **Within the Society:** Your name, flat number, and complaint details are visible to society administrators and assigned workers to fulfill service requests.
- **Service Providers:** We use third-party services (like Google Firebase) for cloud hosting, database management, and authentication. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including Firebase Security Rules and encrypted data transmission, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your local jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data (e.g., updating your phone number via the Admin).
- Request deletion of your data when you leave the society.

To exercise these rights, please contact your society's Management Committee.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your Society Management Committee or email support@societysync.local.
