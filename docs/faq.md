# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. Getting Started

**Q: How do I log in to the SocietySync app?**
A: To log in, open the app and enter your registered mobile number and flat invite code. You will receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS. Enter this code to securely log in. (During the current testing phase, you can use the invite code `123456` and OTP `123456`).

**Q: Where do I get my Invite Code?**
A: Your invite code is provided by the Society Management Committee when your flat is registered in the system.

## 2. Complaints and Issues

**Q: How do I raise a complaint for my flat?**
A: Tap on the **New Complaint** button on the Home Screen. Select a category (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, Cleaning), describe the issue, choose a preferred time slot, and optionally attach a photo. Tap **Submit Complaint**.

**Q: What is a "Society Issue" and how is it different from a Complaint?**
A: A **Complaint** is specific to your personal flat (e.g., a leaking tap in your kitchen). A **Society Issue** is for common areas (e.g., a broken light in the hallway, a damaged bench in the park). Use the "Report Society Issue" button to report common area problems.

**Q: Can I attach a photo to my complaint?**
A: Yes! When creating a complaint or society issue, scroll down to the "Attach Image" section to take a photo using your camera or select one from your gallery.

## 3. Ironing Services

**Q: How does the ironing service work?**
A: You can log a request for ironing from the app. A designated worker will pick up your clothes, iron them based on the rates set by the society, and return them. You can track your outstanding ironing bills via the **My Ironing Bills** section.

## 4. Account and Security

**Q: Who can see my data?**
A: Only the Society Management Admin and the workers assigned to your specific complaints have access to your flat number and issue details. We employ strict data security rules.

**Q: My phone number changed. How do I update it?**
A: Currently, phone numbers must be updated by the Admin. Please contact your Society Management Committee to have them update your registered mobile number in the Admin Dashboard.
